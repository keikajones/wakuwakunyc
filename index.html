What is Waku Waku?

A Japanese expression for excitement and the name of a new Japanese pop culture festival launching this August in Brooklyn. A celebration of anime, manga, music, food, film, fashion, and more, Waku Waku features guests from Japan, interactive exhibits, workshops, exclusives, and premieres. Waku Waku encourages creativity and wants all our attendees to feel "waku waku"!

Where is Waku Waku?

Waku Waku's HQ will be in the brand new Brooklyn Expo Center with panels, screenings, signings, and special events spread over Brooklyn's Greenpoint and Williamsburg neighborhoods. Just a short hop from Manhattan by subway or water taxi, Waku Waku combines Cool Japan with New York's coolest borough.

Who is Waku Waku?

Waku Waku is run by Azix, a Japanese events and marketing firm with offices in Tokyo and NYC and staff with experience running conventions around the world. We've also got a dog named Tucker.
